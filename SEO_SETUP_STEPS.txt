SEO setup steps for Effortless Earn

1) Deploy this ZIP to GitHub/Firebase Hosting.
2) Open these URLs and confirm they work:
   https://effortless-earn-7f39e.web.app/robots.txt
   https://effortless-earn-7f39e.web.app/sitemap.xml
   https://effortless-earn-7f39e.web.app/about.html
   https://effortless-earn-7f39e.web.app/public-offers.html

3) If you use a custom domain, replace https://effortless-earn-7f39e.web.app inside sitemap.xml and robots.txt with your real domain.

4) Go to Google Search Console:
   https://search.google.com/search-console

5) Add your website property.
   Easy method: use URL prefix, for example https://effortless-earn-7f39e.web.app

6) Verify ownership.
   If Google gives an HTML file, upload it to your GitHub project root and deploy.
   If Google gives meta tag, paste it inside <head> of index.html.

7) Submit sitemap:
   sitemap.xml

8) Wait. Indexing can take a few days. Ranking can take weeks/months.

Important:
- Admin/dashboard/wallet/reports are noindex because Google should not index private user pages.
- Public SEO pages are indexable: home, public offers, about, contact, privacy, terms.
